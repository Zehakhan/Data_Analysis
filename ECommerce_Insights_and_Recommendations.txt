
# E-Commerce Sales Insights and Customer Analysis

## Overview
This project is a deep-dive analysis of an e-commerce dataset, revealing key trends in revenue, customer behavior, and product performance over multiple years. By leveraging SQL and Python, we uncover actionable insights to guide strategic decision-making.

---

## Highlights
- **Top Categories & Sellers**: Detailed breakdown of key revenue drivers.
- **Year-over-Year Growth**: Insights into yearly performance fluctuations.
- **Customer Analysis**: Identification of high-value customers across years.
- **Cumulative Sales Trends**: Visualization of growth trajectory over time.

---

## Recommendations
1. **Category Focus**: Increase investment in high-performing categories.
2. **Customer Retention**: Develop loyalty programs for top customers.
3. **Operational Review**: Address periods of declining growth to optimize operations.
4. **Forecasting**: Use cumulative trends for predictive analysis.

---

## Technical Details
- **Language**: SQL and Python
- **Libraries**: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Pandas
- **SQL Features**: Window Functions, Aggregations, Joins
